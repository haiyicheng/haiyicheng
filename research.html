Financial Assets and Cardiometabolic Risk in Early Adulthood

This study examines how financial assets in early adulthood relate to cardiometabolic health outcomes using Add Health data. Applied Poisson regression and generalized structural equation modeling to explore mediation pathways linking financial assets to health.

Does Pressured Internet Use Promote Chinese Older Adults' Mental Health?

Applied confirmatory latent class analysis to distinguish pressured Internet use from voluntary use patterns among older adults. Used structural equation modeling to examine differential associations with mental health outcomes.

Gender Disparities in Grade Retention among Asian Americans

Used ACS microdata (2010-2019) and sibling fixed effects models to examine three-way interactions between gender, race/ethnicity, and family structure. Applied linear probability models with household-clustered standard errors to analyze grade retention patterns.
